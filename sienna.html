Toyota Sienna 2024

The 2024 Toyota Sienna is a minivan that has been redesigned to be more stylish and comfortable, while still offering the versatility that families need.

All Siennas come with a hybrid powertrain that uses a four-cylinder engine and electric motors for a combined output of 245 horsepower. This provides decent power for acceleration and highway cruising, while also delivering impressive fuel economy for a minivan, with estimates around 8.9 L/100km (combined city/highway). Front-wheel drive (FWD) is standard, with all-wheel drive (AWD) available as an option on all trims except the Woodland Edition, where it comes standard.
